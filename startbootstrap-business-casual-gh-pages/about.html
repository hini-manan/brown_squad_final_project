About >US<

We are high school students who went to girls who code (a 7-week program designed to increase the pool of women in STEM).

In girls who code, also known as GWC, we were taught how to code in different computer programming languages including HTML and CSS, JavaScript, Java, and Python.

From the information we were taught during these weeks, we were able to develop an app aiding roommates with managing with money.

What is >Split-Mate<

>Split-Mate< is an app designed to help roommates split money for bills.

This app allows you to create lists of items for each bill, and store or share them, after they are created. It automatically splits charged money based on the number roommates you have. These lists can be viewed on a calendar, based on the day that it was created.
